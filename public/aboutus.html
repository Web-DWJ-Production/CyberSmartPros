About Us
Cyber Smart Pros, LLC (CSP) provides information technology and management consulting services to federal, state, and local governments as well as private sector companies. Its core services are advisory, cybersecurity, and software development services. The Company is a verified Service Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) by the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs. The Virginia Department of Small Business and Supplier Diversity certified the company as a service-disabled veteran, minority-owned, and a micro business.
