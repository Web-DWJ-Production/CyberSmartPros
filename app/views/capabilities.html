Capabilities & Services

Advisory

Our advisory services provide an independent and objective examination of an organization's records and activities to assess (1) the adequacy of their controls to ensure compliance with established laws and regulatory requirements, and (2) the effectiveness of organizational policies and procedures. When necessary, our expert consultants provide advice and recommendations to improve compliance and organizational performance. Our Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) experts are not your typical "IT Geeks". Our GRC experts follow an integrated and forward-thinking approach to help organizations make the right strategic and management decisions in a resource-constrained and complex business environment. Our advisory services include:
  •  FITARA
  •  FISMA
  •  OMB Circular A-130 (Managing Federal Information Resources)
  •  OMB Circular A-123 (Internal Control)
  •  OMB Circular A-11 (Capital Planning and Investment Control (CPIC))
  •  Privacy/Identity Theft
  •  COBIT
  •  NIST Cybersecurity Framework
  •  NIST SP 800-171 Compliance

Cyber Security

Our cybersecurity services are based on the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) Risk Management Framework (RMF) for federal, state, and local governments. For our private sector customers, our services are based on the Center for Internet Security (CIS) Controls and the NIST Cybersecurity Framework. Unlike other cybersecurity practitioners, our “Cyber Smart Pros” recognize there is more to achieving security than being compliant with a framework or buying more security tools to automate. Because our experts take a technology agnostic approach to implementing an organization-wide information-security (Infosec) or Information Assurance (IA) program, we help organizations implement smarter solutions tailored to their unique needs and challenges. To protect organizations from a growing variety of cyber threats and cyber crime, our cybersecurity services include:

  •  Security Awareness Training and Education
  •  Cyber Hygiene Assessments
  •  Confidential Unclassified Information (CUI) Assessments (NIST SP 800-171)
  •  System Security Plan (SSP) Development
  •  Security Control Assessments
  •  Continuous Monitoring
  •  Certification & Accreditation (C&A)
  •  Assessment & Authorization (A&A)
  •  Cloud/FedRAMP
