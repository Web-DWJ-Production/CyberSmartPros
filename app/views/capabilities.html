Capabilities & Services

Advisory

Our advisory services provide an independent and objective examination of an organization's records and activities to assess (1) the adequacy of their controls to ensure compliance with established laws and regulatory requirements, and (2) the effectiveness of organizational policies and procedures. When necessary, our expert consultants provide advice and recommendations to improve compliance and organizational performance. Our advisory services include:
  •  FITARA
  •  FISMA
  •  OMB Circular A-130 (Managing Federal Information Resources)
  •  OMB Circular A-123 (Internal Control)
  •  OMB Circular A-11 (Capital Planning and Investment Control (CPIC))
  •  Privacy/Identity Theft
  •  COBIT
  •  NIST Cybersecurity Framework
  •  NIST SP 800-171 Compliance

Cyber Security

Our cybersecurity services are based on the NIST Risk Management Framework (NIST SP 800-37) for federal, state, and local governments. For our private sector customers, our services are based on the Center for Internet Security (CIS) Controls and the NIST Cybersecurity Framework. Our experts implement an organization-wide Information Security (Infosec) or information assurance (IA) program based on the best framework for each organization. To protect organizations from a growing variety of cyber threats and cyber crime, our cybersecurity services include:
  •  Security Program Implementation
  •  Security Policy Development
  •  Disaster Recovery Planning
  •  Security Awareness Training and Education
  •  Cyber Hygiene Assessments
  •  Confidential Unclassified Information (CUI) Assessments (NIST SP 800-171)
  •  System Security Plan (SSP) Development
  •  Security Control Assessments
  •  Continuous Monitoring
  •  Certification & Accreditation (C&A)
  •  Assessment & Authorization (A&A)
  •  Cloud/FedRAMP
  •  Plan of Action and Milestones (POAM)
  •  Remediation Actions
  •  Risk Vulnerability Assessments (RVA)
  •  Vulnerability Scanning
  •  Penetration Testing

Software Development

Our experts support multiple software development methodologies (Agile, Waterfall, Spiral, Scrum, Kanban, DevOps, etc.) to meet the differing needs of our customers. Regardless of the methodology, our implementation is based on CMMI, PMBOK, and ISO standards along with other best practices to stay on schedule and on budget. Because modernizing legacy IT systems and applications often involve moving data between on-premise/in- house data centers to cloud environments, we perform data conversion and data migration. When customers are looking to achieve dramatic improvements in productivity, cycle times, security, cost or quality, we start with Business Process Reengineering (BPR). Our software development services include:
  •  Project Management
  •  Requirements Analysis
  •  Design and Data Modeling
  •  Development and Programming
  •  IV&V, Software Quality Assurance, and Software Testing
  •  Business Process Reengineering (BPR)
  •  Data Conversion and Data Migration
